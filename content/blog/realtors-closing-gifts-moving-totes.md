## The Problem with Most Closing Gifts

If you're a realtor in Coeur d'Alene, you've probably given your share of closing gifts: wine baskets, candles, cutting boards, custom doormats. They're all nice. But they all have the same problem.

**Your client gets them *after* they've already moved.**

The closing gift arrives at the new house, gets unpacked eventually, and goes on a shelf. It's thoughtful — but it doesn't actually *help* them with the hardest part of the whole home-buying experience: **the move itself**.

## A Closing Gift They'll Actually Remember

Imagine this instead:

Two weeks before closing, your client gets a call from MoverTotes. "Hi! Your realtor wanted to make your move easier — they've gifted you a full package of our moving totes. When would you like them delivered?"

That's a gift that hits at exactly the right moment, solving exactly the right problem, and you're the hero of the entire story.

## Why It Works

### It's Unique

Every realtor gives wine, plants, and gift cards. Nobody gives *the thing that makes moving easier*. You stand out immediately.

### It's Useful

Unlike decorative items, a moving tote rental is something your client needs **right now**. They're actively dreading packing. You just eliminated that dread.

### It's Memorable

Every single tote has your client's name on the gift tag. Every time they pack a box, they're reminded who thought ahead enough to give them this. After the move, when friends ask how the move went, you become part of the story.

### It's Shareable

Your client will tell friends, family, and coworkers about "the realtor who sent us moving boxes." That's the kind of word-of-mouth you can't buy.
